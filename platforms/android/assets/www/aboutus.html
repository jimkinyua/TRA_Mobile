ABOUT US

iSPEC is a mobile application that allows the inspection officer to receive new applicantions, proceed to visit the applicant's premises and carry out the inspection.

He/ she will:

  1. Capture the inspection report using a designated checklist and fill as they carry out the inspection
  2. Also capture photos of the premises and submit along with the inspection checklist.

The Application will also capture the GPS Location of the phone at the time when the checklist is submitted.

It is simple, interactive and acccessible!
